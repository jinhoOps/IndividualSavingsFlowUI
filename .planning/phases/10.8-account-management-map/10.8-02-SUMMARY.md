## Accomplishments

- Added `apps/account-map/modules/map-renderer.js` as a route-local SVG renderer with selectable account nodes, directed relationship edges, stable `data-*` identifiers, type colors, and short labels (`income`, `transfer`, `utility`, `loan`, `savings`, `invest`, etc.).
- Added `apps/account-map/modules/dom.js` for safe DOM rendering of summary counts, selection detail, linked account relationships, and candidate review controls.
- Wired Account Map selection, keyboard activation, candidate accept/exclude, and relationship `paymentDay`/`memo` edits in `apps/account-map/app.js`.
- Extended `AccountMapState` with Account Map-owned candidate acceptance, candidate exclusion, and relationship edit persistence under `isf-account-map-v1`.
- Updated layout CSS so desktop and mobile keep the SVG map as the first-viewport dominant surface while summary counts remain compact.
- Expanded Playwright coverage from 3 to 8 tests for amount hiding, detail reveal, candidate decisions, no Main mutation, unsafe text rendering, no filter tabs, storage round trip, and mobile first-viewport behavior.

## Decisions Made

- Kept overview map labels type-only and count-only; formatted exact amounts are asserted absent from the map until selection.
- Candidate acceptance creates a payment/payee node and a draft relationship inside Account Map state, then removes the candidate from confirmation-needed counts.
- Candidate exclusion removes the candidate from Account Map draft review state without deleting or changing Main data.
- Account detail shows linked incoming/outgoing relationships only; it does not expose ordinary Step 1 income/expense/savings/invest editors.

## Detailed Findings

### Pillar 1: Copywriting (3/4)

**PASS:** The primary modal labels are now `재무설정 상세`, `편집 취소`, and `재무설정 저장`, matching the integrated repair direction.

**PASS:** The forbidden Phase 10.5 couple labels `부부합산`, `본인 설정`, and `배우자 설정` are asserted absent after default render, modal open, save, cancel, and repeated-save cycles.

**PASS:** The automatic savings and adjustment copy is descriptive rather than advisory: it explains available flow and adjustment basis without guilt, blame, partner responsibility, tax, lending, or investment advice framing.

**RESIDUAL:** Some legacy Step 1 terms such as `미래자산 투입률` remain outside the repaired modal. They are not blockers for Phase 10.5 but should be reviewed during broader Step 1 copy cleanup.

### Pillar 2: Visuals (3/4)

**PASS:** The default Step 1 screen remains light: one `재무설정 상세` entry plus existing summary cards before Sankey, with no restored `#householdBudgetPanel`.

**PASS:** Summary cards and category cards all route into the same integrated detail surface, avoiding the Phase 10 split-panel contradiction.

**PASS:** The integrated modal shows ordered tabs for `월 수입`, `월 생활비`, `투자`, `저축`, and `결과/자동 저축`, plus a persistent five-value summary rail.

**RESIDUAL:** No screenshot artifact was captured during this review, so final visual nuance remains code/test verified rather than image-reviewed.

### Pillar 3: Color (3/4)

**PASS:** New Phase 10.5 financial-detail surfaces use flat panels, borders, neutral backgrounds, and restrained status accents.

**PASS:** The over-budget adjustment panel uses a contained warning treatment and does not introduce decorative gradients or one-note palette drift.

**RESIDUAL:** Some legacy unused household overview CSS still contains gradient styling. It is not rendered by Phase 10.5 paths, but a later cleanup can remove it to reduce audit noise.

### Pillar 4: Typography (3/4)

**PASS:** Summary rail values, automatic savings result, variable row metrics, tab labels, and adjustment choices wrap safely with `overflow-wrap` and stable line-height.

**PASS:** Editable money inputs now have stable minimum height and keep tabular numeric alignment for scanning.

**RESIDUAL:** The existing stylesheet still uses several heavy weights inherited from earlier phases. They do not break the Phase 10.5 flow, but a design-system pass could normalize them.

### Pillar 5: Spacing (4/4)

**PASS:** The summary rail stacks at mobile width without document overflow greater than 4px.

**PASS:** Detail tabs wrap to two columns on mobile and keep the final result tab full width.

**PASS:** Variable row detail fields switch to single-column mobile layout, avoiding side-by-side amount compression.

**PASS:** The financial modal pending action bar now stays in the modal layout as sticky content instead of floating over focused inputs.

### Pillar 6: Experience Design (4/4)

**PASS:** The repaired flow supports opening the integrated modal, visiting every tab, editing income/living expense/investment values, expanding a variable row, resolving an excess adjustment, saving, repeated saving, and canceling without durable-state leaks.

**PASS:** Save/cancel remains draft-only until `재무설정 저장`, and repeated saves do not duplicate financial items or Sankey labels.

**PASS:** Variable expense `actualSpent` persists only for variable rows, while fixed rows are sanitized to remove actual-spending fields.

**PASS:** No nested modal appears from the result tab; the savings action navigates within the same `재무설정 상세` modal.
